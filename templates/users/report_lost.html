Report a Lost Item

Follow these instructions to report your lost item and improve your chances of recovery.

Reporting Process

Important Information

The Lost and Found service at our institution is managed by the BAO Office. All lost items must be reported in person at this office.

1

Visit the BAO Office

Go to the BAO Office located in the Student Center building during operational hours to report your lost item.

Office Hours:

Monday - Friday: 9:00 AM - 4:00 PM

Closed on weekends and holidays

2

Complete a Lost Item Report Form

You will need to fill out a form with detailed information about your lost item. Be prepared to provide:

  • A detailed description of the item
  • When and where you believe you lost it
  • Any identifying features or markings
  • Your contact information
  • Student ID or other identification
Download Form Template (Coming Soon)
3

Verification Process

Be prepared to provide proof of ownership if your item is found. This may include:

  • Photos of the item
  • Receipt of purchase
  • Ability to describe unique features not visible from the outside
  • Serial numbers or other identifying information
4

Follow Up

After reporting your lost item:

  • Check the Lost and Found page regularly to see if your item appears
  • Contact the BAO Office periodically to inquire about your lost item
  • You will be notified if an item matching your description is found

Tips to Increase Recovery Chances

Act Quickly

Report your lost item as soon as possible. The sooner you report it, the greater the chances of recovery.

Be Detailed

Provide as much detail as possible about your item, including color, brand, model, and any unique features.

Retrace Your Steps

Try to remember exactly where you last had your item and check all possible locations before reporting.

Register Your Valuables

Consider registering your valuable items with our QR tag system to improve future recovery.
